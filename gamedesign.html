Game Description

Retail Resort Tycoon is a 2-player, competitive game that teaches players how to manage money and run a small-business. Players can build their store layout on their game board using the physical pieces.

Game Pieces

The game includes two of each of the following pieces:

  • A blue scan card
  • A yellow scan card
  • A game board
  • An employee cash register
  • Four regular shelves
    1. A bread shelf
    2. A shampoo shelf
    3. A sunscreen shelf
    4. A towel shelf
  • Three special shelves
    1. A Christmas shelf
    2. A New Year's Shelf
    3. A Valentine's Day shelf
  • A cash register
  • 5-dollar Retail Bucks
  • 10-dollar Retail Bucks
  • 50-dollar Retail Bucks
  • 100-dollar Retail Bucks

Game Directions

Setting up the Game

Each player should begin with:

  • A game board
  • One regular-type shelf
  • Two five-dollar Retail Bucks
  • One ten-dollar Retail Bucks
  • One twenty-dollar Retail Bucks

Taking Turns

On each turn, the player must scan both the yellow card, which generates a regular-type event, and the blue card, which generates a special-type event. The player must complete the action prompted by the generate event (i.e. gaining Retail Bucks or losing Retail Bucks). Players can only gain Retail Bucks from shelves that are on their game board. If the player chooses, they can purchase one shelf or one register for 50 Retail Bucks. Should the player chose to purchase a register, they have hired an employee, and must pay them a 10 Retail Buck-wage every turn. However, players with an employee gain a 5 Retail Buck bonus on every regular transaction and a 15 Retail Buck bonus on every special transaction.

Ending the Game

The game ends after each player has taken 15 turns
